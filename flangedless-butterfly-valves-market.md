<p><p>The flangeless butterfly valves market includes two main types: soft seal valves and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves feature elastomeric seals that provide excellent closure and are ideal for low-pressure applications where leakage is minimal. Conversely, hard seal valves utilize metal-to-metal contact, making them suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature environments. Both types are designed for efficient flow control in various industries, including water treatment, HVAC, and petrochemicals, offering distinct advantages based on the operational requirements.</p></p>
