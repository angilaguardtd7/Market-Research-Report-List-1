<p><p>Veterinary imaging equipment encompasses various technologies used for diagnosing and monitoring animal health. Radiography (X-Ray) systems provide detailed images of bones and internal organs. Ultrasound imaging systems utilize sound waves for real-time visualization of soft tissues and organs. Computed Tomography (CT) offers cross-sectional views for comprehensive assessments. Video endoscopy allows veterinarians to examine internal structures non-invasively. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) delivers high-resolution images of soft tissue, aiding in complex diagnoses. Each type supports improved animal care and treatment precision.</p></p>
