<p><ul><li>Online Remote Sensing</li><li>Offline Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The onboard temperature sensing devices market is categorized into two main types: online remote sensing and offline sensing. Online remote sensing involves real-time monitoring of temperature data through wireless communication systems, allowing for immediate feedback and quick decision-making. In contrast, offline sensing requires physical retrieval of data from the sensors after a specific period, necessitating manual interventions. Each type serves distinct applications, with online systems favored for real-time operational needs and offline systems suited for periodic assessments or environments where connectivity is limited.</p></p>
