<p><p>The Automotive Child Occupant Sensor market encompasses various technologies designed to detect the presence and size of child passengers in vehicles. Ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to measure distance, ensuring accurate detection of occupants. Electric field sensors create a field around the seat and detect changes when a child is present. Capacitive sensors rely on changes in capacitance due to the presence of a body, enabling precise identification. Each type contributes to enhanced safety measures in vehicles by ensuring proper airbag deployment.</p></p>
