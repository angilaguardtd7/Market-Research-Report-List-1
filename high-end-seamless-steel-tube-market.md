<p><p>The high-end seamless steel tube market comprises two main types: hot rolled and cold rolled seamless steel tubes. Hot rolled seamless tubes are manufactured at high temperatures, resulting in a stronger and more durable product suitable for applications requiring high strength, such as in construction and heavy machinery. Conversely, cold rolled seamless tubes are produced at lower temperatures, offering enhanced precision, surface finish, and dimensional accuracy, making them ideal for sectors like automotive and aerospace where tight tolerances are essential.</p></p>
