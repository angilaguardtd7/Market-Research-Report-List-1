<p><p>The flangeless V port ball valves market is characterized by two primary types: soft seal valves and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves utilize elastomeric materials, providing a tight seal and dampening vibrations, making them ideal for low-pressure applications. In contrast, hard seal valves employ metal-to-metal contact, offering enhanced durability and resistance to high pressure and extreme temperatures, suitable for demanding industrial environments. Both types cater to diverse applications, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability in fluid control systems.</p></p>
