<p><p>The optocoupler market is divided into two primary types: non-linear and linear optocouplers. Non-linear optocouplers are designed for applications that require signal isolation while handling varying input signals, often found in digital communication systems. In contrast, linear optocouplers are utilized for precise analog signal transmission, ensuring accurate signal fidelity and low distortion, commonly applied in audio equipment and control systems. Both types play crucial roles in improving circuit isolation and enhancing performance across various industries.</p></p>
