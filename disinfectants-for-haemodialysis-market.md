<p><p>The disinfectants for haemodialysis market consists primarily of chlorine-based and acetic acid-based disinfectants. Chlorine-based disinfectants are highly effective in killing a wide range of pathogens due to their strong oxidation potential, making them suitable for surface disinfection in dialysis settings. Acetic acid-based disinfectants, on the other hand, provide an alternative with less corrosive properties and are effective against biofilms and certain microorganisms. Both types are essential for maintaining a sterile environment, thereby ensuring patient safety during dialysis procedures.</p></p>
